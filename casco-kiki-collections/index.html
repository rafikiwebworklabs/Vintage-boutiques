Our Story

Located in the heart of Nairobi at 54 Casco, Casco Kiki Collections has been curating unique vintage pieces since our founding. We specialize in bringing timeless fashion back to life.

Our boutique offers carefully selected vintage clothing that tells a story. Each piece in our collection has been handpicked for its quality, uniqueness, and style.
